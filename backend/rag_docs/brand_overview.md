# Brand Overview: Aurelio & Finch

**Positioning:**
Aurelio & Finch is a premium contemporary apparel brand dedicated to the modern urban professional. We bridge the gap between high-fashion aesthetics and everyday functional durability.

**Target Audience:**
Professional men and women aged 25–45 who value quality, sustainability, and timeless design over transient trends. Our customers are quality-conscious, detail-oriented, and appreciate the "automation of excellence" in their wardrobe.

**Brand Voice & Tone:**

- **Refined:** We communicate with elegance and precision.
- **Concise:** We respect our customers' time; we are brief but thorough.
- **Confident:** We are experts in our craft and speak with authority.
- **Empathetic:** We understand the challenges of a busy professional life and strive to provide seamless solutions.

**Core Values:**

1. **Architectural Integrity:** Every garment is built with a focus on structure and longevity.
2. **Fabric First:** We source only the finest natural and technical blends.
3. **Adaptive Elegance:** Our pieces transition effortlessly from a morning board meeting to an evening gallery opening.
