# Fabric Education & Care Guide

Aurelio & Finch garments are crafted from superior materials sourced from elite Italian and Japanese mills.

## Core Materials

1. **Merino Extrafine:**
   - **Properties:** Moisture-wicking, odor-resistant, and incredibly soft.
   - **Season:** All-season.
   - **Care:** Hand wash or dry clean only. Never tumble dry.

2. **Technical Silk Blend:**
   - **Properties:** The sheen of silk with the durability of high-grade polyester.
   - **Season:** Spring/Summer.
   - **Care:** Machine wash cold on delicate cycle. Lay flat to dry.

3. **Heavyweight Japanese Cotton:**
   - **Properties:** Crisp, structured, and takes a beautiful patina over time.
   - **Season:** Autumn/Winter.
   - **Care:** Warm wash with like colors. Tumble dry low.

## General Care Principles

- **Rotate Your Wardrobe:** Avoid wearing the same suit or shirt two days in a row to allow fibers to recover.
- **Hanging:** Use wide, contoured hangers for jackets to maintain shoulder structure.
- **Steaming:** We recommend steaming over ironing to preserve fabric integrity and color.
