# Fit Guide: The Aurelio & Finch Silhouette

At Aurelio & Finch, we believe fit is the most critical component of style. Our garments are designed with a "Body-First" philosophy, ensuring comfort without sacrificing a sharp, tailored aesthetic.

## Fit Categories

1. **Slim Fit:**
   - Tapered through the chest and waist.
   - Designed for a modern, contoured look.
   - Recommended for lean to athletic builds.

2. **Tailored Fit:**
   - Our signature fit.
   - Slightly more room than the Slim Fit, but still follows the lines of the body.
   - Best for standard professional frames.

3. **Relaxed Fit:**
   - Straight cut from chest to hem.
   - Prioritizes movement and a draped aesthetic.
   - Ideal for comfort or those with broader builds.

## Measurement Philosophy

We use **Adaptive Sizing**. This means our garments often incorporate a 1-3% technical stretch (Elastane or Lycra blends) to ensure the fit remains consistent throughout a 12-hour workday.

## Recommendation Rules (Deterministic)

- **Height Bias:** If a customer is over 6'2" (188cm), recommend the "Tall" variant if available, otherwise suggest sizing up for sleeve length.
- **Chest vs. Waist:** Always prioritize the chest measurement for jackets and shirts.
- **Stretch Impact:** For products with a "Stretch Level 3", advice the customer to stick to their true size for a tailored look, or size down for a slim look.
