# Shipping & Returns Policy

## Shipping Policy (Domestic & International)

**Processing Time:**
All orders for in-stock items are processed within 24 hours (Monday–Friday).

**US Shipping:**

- **Standard (3-5 days):** $15 or free on orders over $500.
- **Express (1-2 days):** $35.

**International Shipping:**

- We ship to over 50 countries via DHL Express.
- Tariffs and duties are calculated at checkout (DDP - Delivered Duty Paid).

**Proactive Notifications:**
Customers receive a tracking link via email/WhatsApp as soon as the package leaves our Soho warehouse.

---

## Returns & Exchanges

**The 14-Day Window:**
We offer a 14-day return period from the date of delivery. Items must be in original condition with tags attached.

**Exchanges:**
Exchanges for different sizes are free of charge. We provide a pre-paid shipping label.

**Refund Flow:**
Once received at our warehouse, refunds are processed to the original payment method within 3–5 business days.

**Non-Returnable Items:**
Tailored-to-order garments and final sale items cannot be returned or exchanged.
