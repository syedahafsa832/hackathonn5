# Shipping Policy

## Hours of Operation

The online store runs Monday-Friday 8AM-4PM. The online store does not ship on holidays or weekends as Canada Post doesn't pick up on weekends or holidays. If we have everything in stock, orders will usually go out same day or next day. If we do not have stock, a next day transfer is made to ship the following day.

If a product that you have ordered is not available or on back order, you will receive an email from us before your order has been shipped out. We will offer to hold your order & ship everything once the missing product(s) are back in stock. Alternatively we can issue a refund or credit for the missing product(s).

---

## Shipping Time

Everything is shipped expedited, so orders arrive in a timely matter and a tracking number is sent as soon as the parcel is packed. Tracking is available late afternoon/early evening on the same day the order is packed, once Canada Post scans the order in.

---

## Shipping Cost

Orders shipped within Canada with a subtotal of $99 or more before tax qualify for free shipping.

We do not ship outside of Canada currently.

Anytime your subtotal drops below $99 in Canada, this includes using coupons, points or price matching, you will be charged a flat shipping rate charge of $12.99.

**Loyalty Points are not earned on price matched items.**

**ALL PRICES ARE IN CANADIAN CURRENCY.**

We only ship within CANADA.

---

## In-Store Pickup

At this time, no orders are able to be picked up in-store. This feature will be coming in the New Year.
