# TechCorp - Company Profile

## Overview
TechCorp is a leading provider of cloud-based project management and collaboration tools for small to medium-sized businesses. Founded in 2018, we've grown to serve over 250,000 customers worldwide, helping teams streamline their workflows and improve productivity.

## Mission Statement
To empower teams of all sizes to achieve more through intuitive, reliable, and scalable project management solutions that adapt to their unique workflows.

## Products & Services
- **TechCorp Core**: Our flagship project management platform with task management, team collaboration, and reporting features
- **TechCorp Pro**: Advanced features including custom workflows, advanced reporting, and API access
- **TechCorp Enterprise**: Full-featured solution with SSO, advanced security, and dedicated support
- **Integrations Hub**: Connect with popular tools like Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams, and more
- **Mobile Apps**: iOS and Android applications for on-the-go access

## Target Customers
- **Small Businesses**: Teams of 2-10 people needing simple project coordination
- **Mid-Market**: Companies of 10-100 employees requiring advanced features and reporting
- **Enterprises**: Large organizations needing security, compliance, and custom solutions

## Key Features
- **Task Management**: Create, assign, and track tasks with due dates and priorities
- **Team Collaboration**: Real-time messaging, file sharing, and comment threads
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Visual dashboards, productivity reports, and insights
- **Time Tracking**: Built-in time tracking with automatic reporting
- **Custom Workflows**: Drag-and-drop workflow builder for unique processes
- **API Access**: Full REST API for custom integrations
- **Mobile Access**: Native mobile apps for iOS and Android
- **Security**: Enterprise-grade security with SOC 2 compliance
